ABOUT US

Founded in 1954, the UCD Fencing club is the oldest sports club on campus. Whether you're here to compete or pick up a new hobby, UCDFC is open to all members of the UC Davis campus, regardless of experience. If you've never fenced in your life, don't worry! The club provides all equipment and coaching needed to succeed. The club also has all three weapons available - foil, epee, and sabre. As a member of the NCIFL, we compete with 4 other schools in the league. We hope to see you soon!

FAQ

Do I need
my own gear?

Club dues allow club members to participate in practice and compete without owning any of their own equipment. However, it is highly recommended that as you progress with the club, you begin to acquire some of your own equipment.

How much are dues?
Can I try out before committing?

Dues are paid each quarter. The rates are $80 for full time (attending both practices a week) and $40 for half time (once a week). There is also a one time $20 registration fee for Fusion. There are 3 free trial practices.

How do competitions work?
Do I have to compete?

As part of the NCIFL, we have official tournaments where we travel to other campuses such as UCB, SLO, and UCSC. The final tournament of the year is a home tournament at our own gym! It is not mandatory to participate in these tournaments, but they are definitely a fun experience.

What is
practice like?

We start off practice by warming up with a run. After, we do some conditioning and fencing exercises. Finally, we break up into groups and start practice bouts.

How safe is
fencing?

Fencing is considered one of the safest Olympic sports. Only five Olympic sports have lower injury rates than Fencing.

How do I register
as a member?

To register and participate in any sports club, you need to complete both Fusion and DoSportsEasy forms.
